# ImpactEval

ImpactEval is a comprehensive evaluation framework that measures the performance, effectiveness, and real-world impact of your AI systems across multiple dimensions and metrics.

## Key Features

- **Multi-dimensional Metrics**: Evaluate accuracy, fairness, efficiency, and user satisfaction
- **Benchmark Comparisons**: Compare against industry standards and previous versions
- **Impact Assessment**: Measure real-world outcomes and business impact
- **Automated Reporting**: Generate detailed evaluation reports and insights

## Getting Started

Configure your evaluation criteria and metrics to align with your business objectives and AI system goals. Set up automated evaluation pipelines for continuous assessment.

## Use Cases

- Model performance validation before deployment
- A/B testing and model comparison
- Regulatory compliance and audit preparation
- Continuous monitoring and improvement tracking
