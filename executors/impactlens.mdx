# ImpactLens

ImpactLens provides advanced analytics and visualization capabilities that give you deep insights into your AI system's behavior, performance patterns, and user interactions.

## Key Features

- **Interactive Dashboards**: Explore your data through intuitive visualizations
- **Pattern Recognition**: Discover trends and anomalies in AI behavior
- **Root Cause Analysis**: Drill down into issues and performance bottlenecks
- **Custom Views**: Create tailored dashboards for different stakeholders

## Getting Started

Connect your AI systems to ImpactLens to begin collecting and analyzing performance data. Create custom dashboards to monitor the metrics that matter most to your organization.

## Use Cases

- Performance monitoring and optimization
- User behavior analysis and insights
- System health and reliability tracking
- Strategic decision making with data-driven insights
