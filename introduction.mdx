Impact AI is the modern AI Product Management platform designed to help teams build, refine, and deliver impactful AI products with ease.

Our intelligent agents empower AI teams to streamline product workflows, ensure quality, and drive continuous improvement.

- **Control with Laila** - Maintain strategic oversight, enforce policies, and ensure compliance in AI product development.
- **Manage with Max** - Oversee product management, prioritize features, and align lifecycle development with business goals.
- **Evaluate with Iris** - Analyze AI product performance, run experiments, and quantify product improvements.
- **Analyze with Orion** - Extract insights from AI data, conduct impact assessments, and refine decision-making.
- **Align with Sage** - Perform contextual testing to ensure AI quality, align with business goals, and ensure products meet expected outcomes.

By unifying AI product workflows into a single platform, Impact AI eliminates fragmented processes, enhances collaboration, and accelerates iteration cycles.
