## What is Impact AI?

Impact AI provides everything you need to take AI products from development to production:

- **Observability** — Automatic tracing and telemetry for any GenAI application
- **Evaluation** — Continuous quality assessment with LLM-as-a-judge and custom metrics
- **Intelligence** — Real-time insights, trends, and alerts about your AI's performance
- **Governance** — Policy enforcement, compliance tracking, and audit trails

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Your Product">
    Install the Impact SDK in your AI application. It automatically captures traces from OpenAI, Anthropic, Azure, and more.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define Evaluation Modules">
    Create evaluation criteria using Impact Studio or let our agents generate them from your product context.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor & Improve">
    View real-time analytics, receive alerts on quality degradation, and iterate based on insights.
  </Step>
</Steps>
